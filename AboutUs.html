About Us

MoVie Planner was developed by W11-B2.

We are a team based in the School of Computing, National University of Singapore. Our aim is to transform the AddressBook - Level 4 application into a Movie Scheduler that encompasses practical features for real life usage. We target Cinema Managers who schedule the movies in each cinema, and the respective timetable for each of the theaters in the cinemas.
